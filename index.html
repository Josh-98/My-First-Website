Places in Ottawa

Explore the capital

Besides the Mer Bleau, there are many places to go in Ottawa

The capitol of Ottawa is a fun place to be as there is much to see. Besides the picture of Mer Bleau you can go to Gatineau. They have great trails there. The nature in Ottawa is like none other.

There are many places to eat as well such as the ByWard Market, Somerset, Preston, Lansdowne and the Glebe. Good food is everywhere, so your tummy will never rumble. Go out and explore Ottawa's fine cuisine.

Lastly, there are many great activities to keep you busy. You can go axe throwing, kayaking, painting and visit multiple mueseums and art galleries.

Here is a list of Museums to go to

  1. Museum of Civilization
  2. Museum of nature
  3. Museum of Science and Tech
  4. Art Gallery
